PRIVACY POLICY FOR SSR BULLION LIVE RATES
==================================================
Effective Date: July 21, 2026

Welcome to SSR Bullion ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and live rate services.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
--------------------------------------------------
We collect information in the following ways when you use our application:
- Personal Information: When you register for live rates or WhatsApp notifications, we collect information such as your Full Name, WhatsApp Mobile Number, Shop Name, City/Address, and Preferred Language.
- Device & Usage Data: We automatically collect certain non-personal information when you access the app, such as device model, operating system version, Push Notification Player IDs (via OneSignal), and application open events to deliver notifications accurately.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------
We use the collected information for specific operational and communication purposes, including:
- Sending real-time bullion rate alerts, market updates, and rate change notifications directly to your WhatsApp number.
- Delivering push notifications regarding live rate updates.
- Displaying your personalized member profile within the application.
- Responding to customer support inquiries and requests.
- Improving application performance and security.

3. DATA STORAGE & THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------
Your data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with trusted service providers necessary for app functionality:
- Supabase: Cloud database provider used to securely store customer registrations and application rate data.
- OneSignal: Notification service used to deliver real-time push notifications.
- Google Play Services: Underlying app platform services.

4. DATA SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures, including Row-Level Security (RLS) and SSL/TLS encryption, to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. DATA RETENTION & USER RIGHTS
--------------------------------------------------
We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services. You have the right to:
- Access and view the personal information stored in your app profile.
- Reset or remove your local profile data using the in-app "Log Out & Reset" option.
- Request permanent deletion of your registration data from our database by contacting us.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
--------------------------------------------------
Our application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY
--------------------------------------------------
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated "Effective Date". We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
